About AppointmentAI

AppointmentAI was founded in 2022 by a team of sales professionals and AI engineers who were frustrated with traditional lead generation methods. We saw firsthand how businesses wasted thousands of dollars on paid advertising, only to struggle with lead follow-up and conversion. We knew there had to be a better way. By combining cutting-edge artificial intelligence with deep sales expertise, we created a solution that automates the entire appointment-setting process while maintaining the personal touch that prospects expect.
